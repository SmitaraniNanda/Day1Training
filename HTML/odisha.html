About Odisha

Odisha, located on the eastern coast of India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and scenic landscapes. The famous Jagannath Temple in Puri and the Konark Sun Temple are major attractions.

Major Attractions:

  • Puri Jagannath Temple
  • Konark Sun Temple
  • Chilika Lake
  • Simlipal National Park
  • Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves
